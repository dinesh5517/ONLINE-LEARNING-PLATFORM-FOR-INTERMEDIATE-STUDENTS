3. Extraction of Crude Metal

  • Pyrometallurgy: Heat-based extraction
    • Calcination: Heating in limited air (carbonates ‚Üí oxides)
    • Roasting: Heating in excess air (sulfides ‚Üí oxides)
    • Smelting: Heating with flux and reducing agent
  • Hydrometallurgy: Leaching followed by displacement/reduction
  • Electrometallurgy: Electrolytic reduction (for highly reactive metals)

5. Refining of Metals

  • Distillation: For low boiling metals (Zn, Hg)
  • Liquation: For low melting metals (Sn, Pb)
  • Electrolytic refining: For Cu, Al, Ag, Au
    • Anode: Impure metal
    • Cathode: Pure metal
    • Electrolyte: Metal salt solution
  • Zone refining: For semiconductor materials (Si, Ge)
  • Vapour phase refining: Mond process (Ni), van Arkel method (Ti, Zr)

  • Study the major industrial processes for Fe, Al, Cu, and Zn extraction
  • Understand which refining method is suitable for which metal
