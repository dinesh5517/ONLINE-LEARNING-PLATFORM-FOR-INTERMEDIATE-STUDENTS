States of Matter - EAMCET Short Notes

1. Introduction to States of Matter

  • Matter exists in three main states: solid, liquid, and gas
  • Plasma, Bose-Einstein condensates, and other exotic states also exist
  • States differ in intermolecular forces, arrangement, and motion of particles
  • Changes of state: melting, freezing, vaporization, condensation, sublimation, deposition
