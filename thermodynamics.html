Chemical Thermodynamics - EAMCET Short Notes

1. Basic Concepts

  • System: The part of universe under study
  • Surroundings: Everything outside the system
  • Types of Systems:
    • Open: Can exchange both energy and matter
    • Closed: Can exchange only energy
    • Isolated: No exchange of energy or matter
  • State Functions: Properties that depend only on current state (P, V, T, U, H, S, G)
  • Path Functions: Properties that depend on path (q, w)
