Physical World - EAMCET Short Notes

1. Introduction to Physics

  • Physics is the study of nature and its laws.
  • Explains phenomena from galaxies to atoms.
  • Helps develop new technologies (computers, lasers, satellites).

2. Scope and Excitement of Physics

  • Covers all aspects of nature: mechanics to quantum physics.
  • Leads to innovations like electricity and semiconductors.
  • Explains real-world phenomena in a logical way.

4. Fundamental Forces in Nature

  • Gravitational: Weakest but long-range.
  • Electromagnetic: Between charges, stronger than gravity.
  • Strong Nuclear: Strongest, short-range inside nucleus.
  • Weak Nuclear: Responsible for beta decay.

5. Unification of Forces

  • Physics aims to unify all fundamental forces.
  • Example: Electroweak theory (electromagnetic + weak).
